About


Hello! I'm Travis Bare. I am a web developer focusing on front-end technologies. I have an extensive background in education and a dedication to life-long learning. Here are some of the technologies I have worked with: HTML, CSS, Javscript, JQuery, MySQL, MongoDB, Node, Express, React and a little C#. I really enjoy problem solving, technology and computers.

My career started by teaching engineering using computers and later morphed into 3D modeling and animation. I have a lot of experience teaching CAD/3D modeling/Animation and decided to make a change by learning software development. I then started working with a content management system to create and maintain hospital facility and physician websites. I still continue to learn new things daily.
